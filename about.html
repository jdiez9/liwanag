Get To Know More About the Project

Luntiang Liwanag PH is a Philippine-based non-profit organization. It is a recognized consultor of the Department of Energy (DOE) on renewable energy implementation in the Philippines. It aims to provide clean, renewable energy to all regions of the country -- especially those regions without access to conventual electricity lines.
It began as a local grassroots organization in Davao City. It began as a clique interested in renewable sources of energy such as solar panels, before branching out to equal access concerns. It was eventually picked up and recognized by Davao Light Corp. and the rest is history.
